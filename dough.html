How to Make a Homemade Pizza

To make the pizza, we need to first make the pizza crust from the pizza dough. Then we can add the the tomato sauce and cheese.

Pizza Dough Ingredients

  • Yeast (1 packet) instant yeast is preferred.
  • Water( 105ºF – 115ºF)
  • All Purpose flour (white flour)
  • Oil (Olive oil adds the most flavor)
  • Salt
  • Sugar (just a little bit)
  • Cornmeal (optional, but if possible, put on pizza pan)

Equipment

  • Stand Mixer
  • Pizza Pan
  • Pizza cutter

Steps to Prepare Pizza Dough

  1. Mix the dough ingredients together using a stand mixer.
  2. Beat the dough down with your mixer.
  3. Put the dough into a greased mixing bowl, cover the bowl firmly, and place it aside to rise for about 90 minutes.
  4. Punch down the risen dough to get rid of air bubbles. Divide the dough by 2, as 1 half is enough for 1 pizza.
  5. Roll the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees Farenheit.
  7. Look at the toppings tab for more instructions on what do do with the dough.
