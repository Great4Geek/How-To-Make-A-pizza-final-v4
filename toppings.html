How To Make a Homemade Pizza

Toppings

Toppings

  1. Shredded mozzerella cheeze
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. Vegetables(optional)

Steps for the toppings

  1. Use homemade or store bought tomato sauce and spread it all over the pizza dough circle, except for the edges.
  2. Put your toppings on the sauce and dough. First, spread the cheese. Then, add the toppings.
  3. Look at the cooking the pizza tab for the next steps.
