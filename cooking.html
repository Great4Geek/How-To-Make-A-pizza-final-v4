How To Make a Homemade Pizza

How To Cook The Pizza

Here's the fun part!

1. Put the dough with the toppings on the pizza pan.

2. With an oven mitt, slowly put the pizza pan in the oven that is at 475 degrees Farenheit.

3. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, and then when the pizza crust becomes brown, remove with oven mitt.

4. Slice the pizza with the pizza cutter, and then wait till the pizza cools down before serving.
